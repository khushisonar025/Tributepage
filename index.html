Sindhutai Sapkal Mai

Sindhutai Sapkal was an Indian social worker and social activist known particularly for her work in raising orphaned children in India. She was awarded the Padma Shri in 2021 in Social Work category. Wikipedia Born: 14 November 1948, Wardha Died: 4 January 2022, Pune Parents: Abhiman Sathe Awards: Padma Shri Spouse: Shrihari Sapkal Honours: Mother Teresa Awards (2013), Nari Shakti Puraskar (2017), Padma Shri (2021)
